<p><ul><li>Continuous Radio Frequency Dryer</li><li>Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radio Frequency Dryer market includes two main types: Continuous Radio Frequency Dryers and Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryers. Continuous Radio Frequency Dryers operate in a steady process, allowing for uninterrupted drying of materials as they move through the system, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, Intermittent Radio Frequency Dryers operate in batches, providing flexibility for smaller quantities or varying product types. Both types utilize radio frequency energy to efficiently remove moisture from materials, enhancing production efficiency.</p></p>
