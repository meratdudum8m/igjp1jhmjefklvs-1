<p><p>In the coin changers market, there are two primary types: front road coin changers and rear road coin changers. Front road coin changers are typically positioned in easily accessible areas, providing convenience for customers needing immediate change. They serve high-traffic locations, such as train stations and shopping centers. In contrast, rear road coin changers are located in less visible or strategic spots, often serving specific establishments like vending machines or arcade games, catering to niche markets with specialized needs.</p></p>
