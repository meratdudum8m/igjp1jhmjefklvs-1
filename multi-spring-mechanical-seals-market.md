<p><p>Multi spring mechanical seals are essential components in various industries, designed to prevent fluid leakage in rotating equipment. The market is primarily divided into two types: balanced and unbalanced seals. Balanced seals distribute pressure evenly, allowing for higher efficiency and prolonged service life, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, unbalanced seals have a design that can handle lower pressures, often at lower costs, making them ideal for less demanding environments. Both types cater to specific operational needs.</p></p>
